# Certification Request Letter Template

## Request for Investment Information Certification

### Purpose

This template is used by the Plan Administrator or Plan Sponsor to request the certification letter from the custodian, trustee, or insurance carrier that is required for a limited scope audit under ERISA Section 103(a)(3)(C) and 29 CFR 2520.103-8.

---

## Instructions for Use

1. **Print on Plan Sponsor letterhead** (not audit firm letterhead)
2. **Customize all bracketed items** [like this] with specific plan information
3. **Send early** - Request certification within 30 days of plan year-end
4. **Follow up** if not received within 4-6 weeks
5. **Provide copy to auditor** upon receipt

---

## Standard Certification Request Letter

```
[PLAN SPONSOR LETTERHEAD]


[Date]


[Custodian/Trustee/Insurance Carrier Name]
[Department - typically Trust Operations or Retirement Services]
[Address]
[City, State ZIP]


Re: Request for Certification of Investment Information
    [PLAN NAME]
    EIN: [XX-XXXXXXX]
    Plan Number: [XXX]
    Trust/Account Number: [XXXXXXXXXXXXX]
    Plan Year Ended: December 31, [YEAR]


Dear Sir or Madam:

Pursuant to 29 CFR 2520.103-8 of the Department of Labor's Rules and
Regulations for Reporting and Disclosure under the Employee Retirement
Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA), we are requesting certification of
investment information for the above-referenced plan.

CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

Under DOL regulations, the certification must:

1. Be provided by a bank, trust company, or insurance carrier that is
   regulated and supervised and subject to periodic examination by a
   state or federal agency

2. State that the information is complete and accurate

3. State that the information was prepared in accordance with generally
   accepted accounting principles (GAAP)

4. Cover the following information for the plan year ended December 31,
   [YEAR]:
   - Fair value of all investments as of December 31, [YEAR]
   - Investment income (interest, dividends) for the plan year
   - Realized gains and losses on investments sold during the plan year
   - Unrealized appreciation or depreciation in fair value of investments
   - Investment transactions (purchases and sales) during the plan year
   - [If applicable:] Participant loans outstanding as of December 31, [YEAR]

REQUIRED FORMAT

Please provide the certification in a format that includes:

1. Identification of the plan by name and plan year

2. A statement that the information is complete and accurate

3. A statement that the information was prepared in accordance with GAAP

4. Signature by an authorized official of your institution

5. Title of the signing official

6. Date of the certification

SUPPORTING SCHEDULES

Please also provide the following supporting schedules:

□ Schedule of investments held at year-end, showing:
  - Description of each investment
  - Number of shares/units
  - Fair value as of December 31, [YEAR]

□ Schedule of investment income, showing:
  - Interest income
  - Dividend income
  - Other investment income

□ Schedule of realized gains and losses

□ Schedule of investment purchases and sales (or net activity)

□ [If applicable:] Schedule of participant loans outstanding, showing:
  - Beginning balance
  - New loans issued
  - Loan repayments
  - Loan defaults/deemed distributions
  - Ending balance

TIMING

Please provide the certification and supporting schedules by [DATE -
typically 60-90 days after year-end] to allow sufficient time for
completion of the plan audit and Form 5500 filing.

If there are any questions regarding this request, please contact:

[Contact Name]
[Title]
[Phone Number]
[Email Address]

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Sincerely,


_____________________________
[Plan Administrator Name]
[Title]
Plan Administrator
[Company Name]
```

## Follow-Up Request Letter

Use this template when initial request has not been responded to:

```

[Date]


[Custodian/Trustee/Insurance Carrier Name]
[Department]

[City, State ZIP]

SECOND REQUEST

Re: Certification of Investment Information - URGENT
    [PLAN NAME]
    Trust/Account Number: [XXXXXXXXXXXXX]
    Plan Year Ended: December 31, [YEAR]


Dear Sir or Madam:

On [DATE OF ORIGINAL REQUEST], we submitted a request for certification
of investment information for the above-referenced plan. As of the date
of this letter, we have not received the certification.

The certification is required for completion of the plan's annual audit
and Form 5500 filing. The Form 5500 filing deadline is [DATE], and we
need to receive the certification by [DATE] to meet this deadline.

Please provide the certification at your earliest convenience. If there
is an issue preventing issuance of the certification, please contact
[CONTACT NAME] at [PHONE] or [EMAIL] immediately so we can address the
matter.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this urgent request.

Sincerely,


_____________________________
[Plan Administrator Name]
[Title]
```

Use when the initial certification is incomplete or needs additional information:

```
[PLAN SPONSOR LETTERHEAD]


[Date]

[Department]

[City, State ZIP]


Re: Request for Supplemental Certification
    [PLAN NAME]
    Trust/Account Number: [XXXXXXXXXXXXX]
    Plan Year Ended: December 31, [YEAR]


Dear Sir or Madam:

Thank you for providing the certification letter dated [DATE] for the
above-referenced plan. Upon review, we have identified that the following
items require additional certification or clarification:

[Check applicable items and describe:]

□ The certification does not include [specific item - e.g., realized
  gains/losses, investment transactions, participant loans]

□ The certification period does not cover the full plan year
  (certification covers [PERIOD], plan year is [PERIOD])

□ The certification is missing the required GAAP statement

□ The certification is missing the required completeness statement

□ The following investments are not included in the certification:
  [List investments]

□ Other: [Describe issue]

Please provide a supplemental certification addressing the items noted
above, or an amended certification that includes all required information.

If you have any questions, please contact [CONTACT NAME] at [PHONE].

Thank you for your assistance.

Sincerely,


_____________________________
[Plan Administrator Name]
[Title]
```

The [PLAN NAME] holds a portion of its investments with your institution.
Pursuant to 29 CFR 2520.103-8, we are requesting certification of

[List specific investments or investment types held with this custodian]

who will provide separate certification for those assets. Your certification
should cover ONLY the investments held in the account(s) listed above.

## Sample Acceptable Certification Language

The following language satisfies DOL requirements. Certification from your custodian should include similar statements:

```
We hereby certify that the following information concerning [Plan Name]
for the plan year ended December 31, [YEAR] is complete and accurate
as of the certification date:

- Fair value of investments as of December 31, [YEAR]
- Investment income (dividends, interest) for the plan year
- Realized gains and losses on investments sold during the plan year
- Unrealized appreciation and depreciation in fair value of investments
- Investment transactions (purchases and sales) during the plan year

This information has been prepared in accordance with generally accepted
accounting principles.

[Institution Name] is a [bank/trust company/insurance carrier] regulated
by [regulatory agency] and subject to periodic examination by such agency.

____________________________
[Authorized Signature]
[Title]
[Date]
```

## Common Reasons Certification Is Rejected

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Not from qualifying institution | Identify bank/trust that serves as custodian |
| Missing GAAP statement | Request revised certification |
| Wrong plan year | Request certification for correct period |
| Missing required elements | Request supplemental certification |
| Unsigned or no title | Request signed certification from authorized official |
| Partial year coverage | Request full-year certification or full scope on gap period |
