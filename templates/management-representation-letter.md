# Management Representation Letter Template

## Limited Scope 401(k) Plan Audit

### Professional Standards Reference

This management representation letter template complies with:
- AU-C Section 580, *Written Representations*
- SAS No. 136, *Forming an Opinion and Reporting on Financial Statements of Employee Benefit Plans Subject to ERISA*
- AICPA Professional Standards

---

## Instructions for Use

1. **Print on Plan Sponsor letterhead** (not audit firm letterhead)
2. **Customize all bracketed items** [like this] with specific plan information
3. **Date the letter** as of the date of the auditor's report
4. **Obtain signatures** from both the plan administrator and appropriate financial officer
5. **Retain signed original** in audit file

---

## Management Representation Letter Template

```
[PLAN SPONSOR LETTERHEAD]


[Date of Auditor's Report]


[Audit Firm Name]
[Address]
[City, State ZIP]


This representation letter is provided in connection with your audit of
the financial statements of the [PLAN NAME] (the "Plan"), which comprise
the statements of net assets available for benefits as of December 31,
[YEAR] and [YEAR-1], and the related statements of changes in net assets
available for benefits for the years then ended, and the related notes
to the financial statements, for the purpose of expressing an opinion as
to whether the financial statements are presented fairly, in all material
respects, in accordance with accounting principles generally accepted in
the United States of America (U.S. GAAP).

We confirm that we are responsible for the following:

GENERAL REPRESENTATIONS
-----------------------

1. The fair presentation of the financial statements referred to above
   in accordance with U.S. GAAP.

2. The design, implementation, and maintenance of internal control
   relevant to the preparation and fair presentation of financial
   statements that are free from material misstatement, whether due
   to fraud or error.

3. Providing you with:
   a. Access to all information of which we are aware that is relevant
      to the preparation and fair presentation of the financial
      statements, such as records, documentation, and other matters
   b. Additional information that you have requested from us for the
      purpose of the audit
   c. Unrestricted access to persons within the Plan from whom you
      determined it necessary to obtain audit evidence

4. All transactions have been recorded in the accounting records and
   are reflected in the financial statements.

5. We have disclosed to you the results of our assessment of the risk
   that the financial statements may be materially misstated as a
   result of fraud.

6. We have no knowledge of any fraud or suspected fraud that affects
   the Plan and involves:
   a. Management
   b. Employees who have significant roles in internal control
   c. Others where the fraud could have a material effect on the
      financial statements

7. We have no knowledge of any allegations of fraud, or suspected fraud,
   affecting the Plan's financial statements communicated by employees,
   former employees, participants, regulators, or others.

8. We have disclosed to you all known instances of noncompliance or
   suspected noncompliance with laws and regulations whose effects
   should be considered when preparing the financial statements.

9. We are not aware of any pending or threatened litigation, claims,
   or assessments, or unasserted claims or assessments that are required
   to be accrued or disclosed in the financial statements in accordance
   with U.S. GAAP, and we have not consulted a lawyer concerning
   litigation, claims, or assessments, except as follows:
   [List any litigation or state "None"]

10. We have disclosed to you the identity of the Plan's related parties
    and all the related party relationships and transactions of which
    we are aware.

PLAN ADMINISTRATION REPRESENTATIONS
-----------------------------------

11. We have made available to you a copy of the most recent and currently
    applicable plan document, including all amendments thereto.

12. We believe the Plan is qualified under the applicable provisions of
    the Internal Revenue Code and has received or applied for a
    determination letter from the Internal Revenue Service. The Plan
    has been operated in accordance with the plan document and the
    applicable provisions of the Internal Revenue Code, and the Plan's
    tax-exempt status has not been revoked.

13. The Plan is subject to the provisions of the Employee Retirement
    Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA) and has complied with ERISA
    in all material respects.

14. The Plan and the Plan Sponsor have complied with the fidelity bonding
    requirements of ERISA Section 412.

15. The Plan has filed the required Form 5500 series reports for all
    prior years.

16. Participant data including demographic and payroll data used in
    plan administration and testing is complete and accurate.

17. The Plan has properly administered the provisions of the plan
    document, including eligibility, contributions, vesting, and
    distributions.

CONTRIBUTION REPRESENTATIONS
----------------------------

18. Participant contributions have been remitted to the trust within
    the time frame prescribed by Department of Labor regulations
    (generally as soon as they can reasonably be segregated from the
    Plan Sponsor's general assets). [If late contributions occurred,
    add: "Except as disclosed in the financial statements and
    supplemental schedules."]

19. Employer contributions have been made in accordance with the
    provisions of the plan document and have been deposited within the
    time frame necessary to be deductible for the Plan Sponsor's
    tax year.

20. Contributions have been allocated to participant accounts in
    accordance with the plan document.

21. The Plan has not incurred any contributions in excess of the
    limitations imposed by IRC Section 415.

22. Forfeitures have been accounted for and utilized in accordance
    with the provisions of the plan document.

DISTRIBUTION REPRESENTATIONS
----------------------------

23. Benefit payments and distributions have been made in accordance
    with the provisions of the plan document and applicable
    regulations.

24. Required minimum distributions have been made to all participants
    who have attained the applicable age and terminated employment.

25. Hardship withdrawals, if any, have been made only for qualifying
    events and have been properly documented.

26. All distributions have been properly reported on Forms 1099-R.

27. Required withholdings have been applied to all taxable distributions.

INVESTMENT REPRESENTATIONS
--------------------------

28. We have elected to have the audit conducted as a limited scope
    audit under 29 CFR 2520.103-8 of the Department of Labor's Rules
    and Regulations for Reporting and Disclosure under ERISA.

29. We have instructed you not to perform, and you did not perform,
    any auditing procedures with respect to the investment information
    certified by [CUSTODIAN/TRUSTEE NAME], the Plan's custodian/trustee,
    except for comparing such information with the related financial
    statements.

30. [CUSTODIAN/TRUSTEE NAME] is a bank, trust company, or insurance
    carrier that is regulated and supervised by a state or federal
    agency and subject to periodic examination, and therefore qualifies
    to certify investment information under DOL regulations.

31. The certification from [CUSTODIAN/TRUSTEE NAME] that we provided
    to you meets the requirements of 29 CFR 2520.103-8.

32. All investments were made in accordance with the provisions of the
    plan document and the Plan's investment policy.

33. We believe that the investment valuations provided by the
    custodian/trustee are appropriate for the types of investments
    held by the Plan.

PARTICIPANT LOAN REPRESENTATIONS
--------------------------------

34. Participant loans have been made and administered in accordance
    with the plan document and IRC Section 72(p).

35. No participant loan exceeds the lesser of $50,000 (reduced by
    certain prior outstanding loans) or 50% of the participant's
    vested account balance.

36. All participant loans provide for level amortization over the
    term of the loan.

37. Loan defaults have been properly identified and deemed distributions
    have been properly processed and reported.

38. Participant loans outstanding are collectible at the amounts
    recorded in the financial statements.

COMPLIANCE TESTING REPRESENTATIONS
----------------------------------

39. The Plan has satisfied the nondiscrimination requirements of
    IRC Sections 401(a)(4), 401(k), and 401(m), or has corrected
    any failures in accordance with EPCRS.

40. The Plan has satisfied the coverage requirements of IRC Section 410(b).

41. The Plan has performed or will perform top-heavy testing, and
    if top-heavy, the required minimum contribution has been or
    will be made.

42. [For Safe Harbor Plans:] The Plan has satisfied all requirements
    to maintain safe harbor status, including the timely distribution
    of safe harbor notices.

PROHIBITED TRANSACTION REPRESENTATIONS
--------------------------------------

43. We are not aware of any prohibited transactions with parties in
    interest as defined in ERISA Section 3(14) and IRC Section
    4975(e)(2), except for transactions that are exempt under
    ERISA Section 408 or IRC Section 4975(d). [If prohibited
    transactions occurred, list them.]

44. [If late contributions occurred:] The late remittance of
    participant contributions disclosed in the financial statements
    constitutes a prohibited transaction. We are in the process
    of correcting this transaction through [the DOL Voluntary
    Fiduciary Correction Program / other correction method].

SUBSEQUENT EVENTS
-----------------

45. No events have occurred subsequent to the date of the financial
    statements and through the date of this letter that would require
    adjustment to or disclosure in the financial statements.

46. [List any subsequent events, or state: "We are not aware of any
    significant subsequent events."]

FINANCIAL STATEMENT REPRESENTATIONS
-----------------------------------

47. The financial statements referred to above are fairly presented in
    conformity with U.S. GAAP.

48. We believe that the significant assumptions used in making
    accounting estimates are reasonable.

49. Related party relationships and transactions have been appropriately
    accounted for and disclosed in accordance with U.S. GAAP.

50. All events subsequent to the date of the financial statements and
    for which U.S. GAAP requires adjustment or disclosure have been
    adjusted or disclosed.

51. The effects of uncorrected misstatements are immaterial, both
    individually and in the aggregate, to the financial statements
    as a whole. A list of the uncorrected misstatements is attached
    to this letter. [Attach list or state "There are no uncorrected
    misstatements."]

52. We are in agreement with the adjusting journal entries you have
    recommended, and they have been posted to the Plan's accounts.
    [Or state: "There were no adjusting entries proposed."]

53. We have disclosed to you all known actual or possible litigation
    and claims whose effects should be considered when preparing the
    financial statements.

SUPPLEMENTAL SCHEDULE REPRESENTATIONS
-------------------------------------

54. The supplemental schedules are fairly stated in all material
    respects in relation to the financial statements as a whole.

55. The Schedule of Assets (Held at End of Year) includes all
    investments held by the Plan at year-end, including participant
    loans, and party-in-interest transactions have been properly
    identified.

56. [If applicable:] The Schedule of Delinquent Participant
    Contributions accurately reports all late contributions and
    lost earnings.
```

## Additional Representations by Situation

### For Plans with Stable Value Investments

Add to investment representations:

```
__. The contract value of the Plan's stable value investments
    represents the relevant measurement attribute for that portion
    of the net assets available for benefits attributable to fully
    benefit-responsive investment contracts.

__. We have evaluated the stable value fund's benefit-responsive
    contracts and believe they meet the criteria required by ASC 962.
```

### For Plans with Employer Securities

Add to investment representations:

```
__. The fair value of employer securities has been determined in
    accordance with the provisions of the plan document and applicable
    valuation standards.

__. [For closely held company stock:] The valuation of employer
    securities was performed by [VALUATION FIRM], a qualified
    independent appraiser, and we believe the methodology and
    assumptions used are appropriate.
```

### For Plans with Real Estate or Alternative Investments

Add to investment representations:

```
__. Real estate and alternative investments have been valued at
    fair value using appropriate valuation methods.

__. We have disclosed to you all information regarding such
    investments, including restrictions on transferability.
```

### For Plans with Corrective Distributions

Add to compliance representations:

```
__. Corrective distributions were made in the amount of $[AMOUNT]
    to correct [ADP/ACP/other] test failures. These distributions
    were properly calculated and distributed within the required
    time frame.
```

### For Plans Under IRS or DOL Examination

Add to general representations:

```
__. We have disclosed to you that the Plan is currently under
    examination by [IRS/DOL]. We have provided you with all
    correspondence related to this examination. The current status
    of the examination is [describe status].
```

### For Plan Mergers

Add to general representations:

```
__. The Plan merged with [OTHER PLAN NAME] effective [DATE].
    All assets and liabilities were properly transferred and
    recorded, and the merger was accomplished in accordance
    with ERISA requirements.
```

### For Plan Terminations

Add to general representations:

```
__. The Plan is in the process of terminating. All required notices
    have been provided to participants and regulatory agencies.
    All assets will be distributed to participants upon final
    determination.
```
