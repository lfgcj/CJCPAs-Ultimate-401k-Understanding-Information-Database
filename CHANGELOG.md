|-----------|-------------|--------------|

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Key Audit Alerts for 2025

1. **Catch-Up Contribution Complexity**: With the new enhanced catch-up for ages 60-63, testing becomes more complex. Verify:
   - Participants age 50-59: $7,500 catch-up limit
   - Participants age 60-63: $11,250 catch-up limit
   - Participants age 64+: $7,500 catch-up limit

2. **HCE Catch-Up Roth Requirement**: Originally effective 2024, delayed to **2026**. For 2025, HCEs can still make pre-tax catch-up contributions.

3. **Automatic Enrollment Testing**: For plans established after December 29, 2022:
   - Initial deferral: 3-10%
   - Annual escalation: 1% per year up to 10-15%
   - Exceptions: Small employers (<10), new employers (<3 years), church plans, governmental plans

4. **Long-Term Part-Time Testing**:
   - 2024 plan year: 3-year rule (1,000 hours threshold)
   - 2025 plan year: 2-year rule (500 hours threshold)
   - Test eligibility for LTPT employees
