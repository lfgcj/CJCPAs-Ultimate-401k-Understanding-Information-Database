# Form 5500 Filing Requirements

## Overview

Form 5500 is the annual return/report that employee benefit plans must file with the Department of Labor, IRS, and PBGC. Understanding Form 5500 requirements is essential for plan compliance.

## Form 5500 Basics

### Purpose

Form 5500 serves multiple purposes:
- Annual report to federal agencies
- Public disclosure document
- Compliance verification tool
- Statistical data collection

### Regulatory Authority

| Agency | Role |
|--------|------|
| DOL | Fiduciary, disclosure, prohibited transactions |
| IRS | Tax qualification requirements |
| PBGC | Plan termination insurance (DB plans) |

### Filing Method

All Form 5500 filings must be made electronically through EFAST2:
- Electronic Filing Acceptance System 2
- Web-based filing available
- Software providers available
- Paper filing not accepted

## Who Must File

### General Requirement

Pension benefit plans must file Form 5500 annually if:
- Plan has assets
- Plan is still in existence
- Plan is not exempt

### Plans Required to File

| Plan Type | Filing Required |
|-----------|----------------|
| 401(k) plans | Yes |
| Profit sharing plans | Yes |
| Money purchase plans | Yes |
| Defined benefit plans | Yes |
| ESOPs | Yes |
| 403(b) plans (subject to ERISA) | Yes |

### Exempt Plans

| Plan Type | Exemption |
|-----------|-----------|
| Governmental plans | Exempt |
| Church plans (non-electing) | Exempt |
| Plans with no employees | May be exempt |
| Unfunded excess benefit plans | Exempt |
| Top-hat plans | Simplified filing |

### One-Participant Plans

Owner-only plans (including spouse):
- No Form 5500 if assets under $250,000
- Form 5500-EZ if assets $250,000 or more
- Can file electronically or on paper

## Form 5500 vs 5500-SF

### Form 5500 (Long Form)

**Required for:**
- Large plans (100+ participants)
- Plans not eligible for 5500-SF
- Plans with certain investments
- Plans requiring audit

**Includes:**
- Main form plus schedules
- Financial statements
- Audit report

### Form 5500-SF (Short Form)

**Eligible if:**
- Fewer than 100 participants at beginning of plan year
- 100% of assets in "eligible plan assets"
- Satisfies additional requirements
- No audit required

**Eligible Plan Assets Include:**
- Qualifying employer securities
- Participant loans
- Insurance contracts
- Bank accounts and CDs
- Shares in registered investment companies
- Collective trust assets

## Filing Deadlines

### Standard Deadline

| Plan Year End | Due Date |
|---------------|----------|
| December 31 | July 31 |
| Other dates | 7 months after plan year end |

### Extension

**Form 5558:**
- Automatic 2.5 month extension
- Must file by original due date
- Calendar year plans: Extended to October 15

**Business Tax Return Extension:**
- Plan may rely on employer's tax extension
- If plan year same as employer fiscal year
- Requires employer to have valid extension

### Final Filing

When plan terminates:
- File final Form 5500
- Mark as final return
- File by normal deadline for final year
- All assets must be distributed

## Required Schedules

### Schedule A: Insurance Information

Required if plan has:
- Insurance contracts
- Provides insurance benefits
- Holds insurance investments

### Schedule C: Service Provider Information

Required for large plans to report:
- Service providers receiving $5,000+
- Accountants and actuaries
- Direct and indirect compensation

### Schedule D: DFE Participation

Required if plan participates in:
- Master trust
- 103-12 Investment Entity
- Group Insurance Arrangement

### Schedule G: Financial Transactions

Required to report:
- Loans or fixed income obligations in default
- Leases in default
- Nonexempt transactions

### Schedule H: Financial Information

Required for large plans:
- Plan assets and liabilities
- Income and expenses
- Compliance questions
- Accountant information

### Schedule I: Financial Information (Small Plan)

For small plans not filing Schedule H:
- Simplified financial information
- Abbreviated compliance questions

### Schedule R: Retirement Plan Information

Required to report:
- Distributions
- Funding information
- Participant counts
- Plan characteristics

### Schedule SB/MB: Actuarial Information

For defined benefit plans only.

## Line-by-Line Guide

### Part I: Annual Report Identification Information

| Line | Information |
|------|------------|
| A | Plan year dates |
| B | Amended return checkbox |
| C | Plan number |
| D | Filing type (5500, 5500-SF, etc.) |

### Part II: Basic Plan Information

| Line | Information |
|------|------------|
| 1a | Plan name |
| 1b | Plan number (3-digit) |
| 1c | Effective date |
| 2a-c | Plan sponsor information |
| 3a-c | Plan administrator information |
| 4 | Employer EIN |

### Part III: Participant Information

| Line | Information |
|------|------------|
| 5 | Total participants at beginning of year |
| 6 | Active participants at beginning of year |
| 7 | Participants with account balances at end of year |
| 8 | Number receiving benefits during year |
| 9 | Total participants at end of year |

### Part IV: Plan Characteristics

Checkboxes for:
- Plan type (401(k), profit sharing, etc.)
- Plan features (automatic enrollment, safe harbor, etc.)
- Special features (ESOP, multiemployer, etc.)

### Part V: Financial Information

Summary totals from:
- Schedule H or Schedule I
- Assets, liabilities, income, expenses
- Net plan assets

### Part VI: Required Attachments

Identify:
- Schedules attached
- Audit report attached
- Other attachments

## Common Filing Errors

### Participant Count Errors

| Error | Issue |
|-------|-------|
| Undercounting | Miss terminated participants with balances |
| Overcounting | Include participants already paid out |
| Wrong date | Using end of year instead of beginning |

### Financial Information Errors

| Error | Issue |
|-------|-------|
| Not reconciling | Doesn't match audit report |
| Wrong valuation | Not fair value |
| Missing items | Incomplete schedules |

### Schedule Errors

| Error | Issue |
|-------|-------|
| Missing schedules | Not attaching required schedules |
| Wrong schedule | Using Schedule I when H required |
| Incomplete schedules | Not listing all required information |

### Timing Errors

| Error | Issue |
|-------|-------|
| Late filing | Missing deadline |
| Early filing | Filing before year end |
| Extension not filed | Assuming automatic extension |

## Late Filing Penalties

### DOL Penalties

**ERISA Section 502(c)(2):**
- $250 per day
- Maximum $150,000 per plan year
- Applies to delinquent filers

### IRS Penalties

**IRC Section 6652(e):**
- $250 per day
- Maximum $150,000 per plan year
- May waive for reasonable cause

### Penalty Abatement

May request abatement if:
- Reasonable cause shown
- First-time late filer
- Corrected promptly
- Good faith efforts demonstrated

### DFVCP (Delinquent Filer Voluntary Compliance Program)

Reduced penalties for voluntary compliance:

| Plan Type | Per Day | Maximum |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Small plan | $10 | $750 per year |
| Large plan | $10 | $2,000 per year |
| Small plan (multiple years) | $10 | $1,500 total |
| Large plan (multiple years) | $10 | $4,000 total |

## Correcting Filed Returns

### Amended Return

To correct previously filed 5500:
- File amended Form 5500
- Check "Amended Return" box
- Attach explanation of changes
- File as soon as error discovered

### When to Amend

Amend if:
- Material errors in amounts
- Missing schedules
- Incorrect participant counts
- Wrong plan characteristics
- Missing signatures

### Don't Amend For

- Immaterial errors
- Items that can be corrected next year
- After DOL/IRS contact about issue

## Form 5500-EZ

### Who Files

One-participant plans (owner and spouse only) with:
- Assets of $250,000 or more at end of year
- Assets less than $250,000 at end of year but filing final return
- Plan terminating regardless of asset value

### Filing Options

- Electronic via EFAST2
- Paper filing permitted (exception to electronic requirement)

### Contents

Simplified version:
- Basic plan information
- Asset totals
- No schedules required

---

## Electronic Filing Requirements

### EFAST2 System

All Form 5500 (not 5500-EZ) must be filed through EFAST2:
- Web-based interface available
- Compatible software available
- PDF attachments required for audit reports
- Signature using EFAST2 PIN

### Attachments

Required attachments:
- Audit report (PDF)
- Financial statements (PDF)
- Supplemental schedules (PDF)
- Other required documents

### Signatures

- Plan administrator must sign
- Can sign electronically via EFAST2
- PIN required for electronic signature
