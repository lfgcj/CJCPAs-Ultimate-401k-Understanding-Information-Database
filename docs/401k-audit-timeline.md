## Key Deadline Summary

### Calendar Year Plan Deadlines

| Deadline | Date | Description | Penalty for Missing |
|----------|------|-------------|---------------------|
| Year-end valuations | Dec 31 | Investment valuations | N/A |
| Certification | Jan-Mar | Obtain from custodian | Delays audit |
| ADP/ACP corrections | Mar 15 | QNEC correction deadline | Failed testing |
| ADP/ACP refunds | Mar 15 | Refund HCE excess | 10% excise tax |
| Employer contribution | Apr 15 (ext: Oct 15) | Tax deduction deadline | Lost deduction |
| Audit completion | Jun-Jul | Issue audit report | Late filing |
| Form 5500 | Jul 31 | Annual report filing | $250/day penalty |
| Extension (Form 5558) | Jul 31 | Request 2.5 month extension | Cannot extend |
| SAR distribution | Sep 30 | To participants | $110/day/participant |
| Extended Form 5500 | Oct 15 | If extension filed | $250/day penalty |
| 404a-5 disclosures | Annual | Participant fee disclosure | DOL penalties |

### Non-Calendar Year Plan Adjustment

For plans with fiscal year-ends other than December 31:

| Event | Timing |
|-------|--------|
| Plan year-end | [Your date] |
| Certification obtained | Within 60 days of year-end |
| Audit fieldwork | Months 3-5 after year-end |
| Form 5500 due | 7 months after year-end |
| Extension deadline | 7 months after year-end |
| Extended deadline | 9.5 months after year-end |
| SAR due | 9 months after year-end |

## Form 5500 Filing Calendar

### Filing Requirements Matrix

| Plan Size | Form | Audit Required? | Standard Deadline | Extended Deadline |
|-----------|------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 100+ participants | 5500 | Yes (large plan) | 7 months | 9.5 months |
| <100 participants | 5500-SF | No (small plan) | 7 months | 9.5 months |
| One participant | 5500-EZ | No | 7 months | 9.5 months |

### Calendar Year Filing Dates

| Item | Standard | Extended |
|------|----------|----------|
| Form 5500 due | July 31 | October 15 |
| Form 5558 extension due | July 31 | N/A |
| SAR distribution | September 30 | December 15 (if extended) |

### Electronic Filing Requirements

All Form 5500 filings must be filed electronically through EFAST2:
- Website: https://www.efast.dol.gov
- Requires EFAST2 credentials
- Audit report attached as PDF
- Financial statements attached as PDF

## Critical Path Tasks

### Tasks That Cannot Be Delayed

| Task | Dependency | Impact if Delayed |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| Obtain certification | Audit cannot complete | Filing deadline missed |
| Complete ADP/ACP testing | March 15 deadline | Excise tax, plan disqualification |
| Issue audit report | Form 5500 filing | Penalties |
| File Form 5500 | July 31 deadline | $250/day penalty |
| File extension | July 31 deadline | Cannot extend |
| Distribute SAR | September 30 | $110/day/participant |

## Responsible Party Matrix

### RACI Matrix for 401(k) Audit

| Task | Plan Sponsor | TPA | Recordkeeper | Custodian | Auditor |
|------|--------------|-----|--------------|-----------|---------|
| Year-end close | R | C | A | C | I |
| Provide census data | A | R | C | - | I |
| Compliance testing | I | R | A | - | I |
| Obtain certification | R | C | - | A | I |
| Provide PBC items | A | R | R | R | I |
| Conduct audit | I | C | C | C | R |
| Prepare financials | A | C | - | - | R |
| Review financials | R | C | - | - | A |
| File Form 5500 | A | R | - | - | C |
| Distribute SAR | R | A | - | - | I |

**Legend:**
- R = Responsible (does the work)
- A = Accountable (owns the outcome)
- C = Consulted (provides input)
- I = Informed (kept updated)

## Extension Procedures

### Form 5558 Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Form | Form 5558 |
| Filing deadline | Original Form 5500 due date |
| Extension period | 2.5 months |
| Automatic? | Yes, if properly filed |
| E-filing | Through EFAST2 |

### Extension Checklist

| Step | Task | Completed |
|------|------|-----------|
| 1 | Determine extension is needed | ☐ |
| 2 | Prepare Form 5558 | ☐ |
| 3 | File by original deadline (July 31 for calendar year) | ☐ |
| 4 | Confirm receipt from EFAST2 | ☐ |
| 5 | Note extended deadline (October 15) | ☐ |
| 6 | Communicate to all parties | ☐ |

### Corporate Tax Extension Linkage

If the plan sponsor files for a corporate tax extension (Form 7004), the Form 5500 deadline is automatically extended to the extended corporate tax due date, but not later than 9.5 months after plan year-end.
